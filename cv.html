Who am I?

I am a statistician who works focused on building models for top priority real problems.

My research focuses on building consistent state-of-the-art models to answer scientific question. I apply Statistics to a real context which makes me feel that my work is useful to society.

I worked as a researcher at the Centre for the Research and Technology of Agroenvironmentaç and Biological Sciences (CITAB) at the University of Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro, with affiliation at the Department of Mathematics, Vila Real, Portugal.

In my period as a permanent assistant professor at the Federal Rural University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRRJ), Brazil. I was invited as a lecturer at the Graduate Program in Environmental and Forest Sciences (PPGCAF) in the UFRRJ and as a senior researcher at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) in the COPPE-SAGE lab dedicated to Sustainable Production Systems since 2018, Brazil.

I have worked as a postdoctoral researcher in the Conservation in Socio-Ecological Systems group at the University of Lisboa in the Centre for Ecology, Evolution and Environmental Changes, CE3C (2021). Posdoctoral research in Ecology at PPGE in the Biology Istitute at UFRJ, Brazil.
